# iPhone UDID ni qanday olish

## Usul 1: iTunes/Finder orqali (Mac bor bo'lsa)
1. iPhone ni Mac ga ulang
2. Finder → iPhone → klik qiling serial number ga → UDID ko'rinadi
3. Nusxa oling

## Usul 2: udid.io veb-sayt orqali (Mac yo'q, eng oson)
1. iPhone da Safari brachzerini oching
2. https://get.udid.io saytiga kiring
3. "Tap to find your UDID" tugmasini bosing
4. Profil o'rnatishga ruxsat bering (bu zararli emas, faqat UDID ko'rsatadi)
5. UDID ni nusxa oling

## Usul 3: iMazing (bepul versiya)
1. iMazing ni yuklab oling (Windows/Mac)
2. iPhone ni ulang
3. Device info → UDID

## UDID format qanday ko'rinadi:
00008110-000A1234B5C6789A  (yangi format)
yoki
a1b2c3d4e5f6a1b2c3d4e5f6a1b2c3d4e5f6a1b2  (eski format, 40 belgi)
